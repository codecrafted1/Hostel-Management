Help Center

Q1: How do I register for HostelHub?

Go to the Login section and click on “Register”. Fill in your student ID and basic details to create your account.

Q2: How can I report a complaint?

Click the “Complaints” card and fill in the form. Your issue will be directly sent to the hostel maintenance team.

Q3: How do I change my mess menu preferences?

Navigate to the “Meal Menu” section and choose the “Customize Menu” option.

Q4: Who can I contact for urgent issues?

You can reach out to the warden via warden@hostelhub.com or call the hostel help desk during working hours.

Q5: How can I view hostel announcements?

Announcements are regularly updated in the “Announcements” card or dashboard section.
